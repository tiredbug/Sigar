Welcome to APAM Barabai

APAM Barabai - is a free and open source Patient Mobile APP for SIMKES Khanza. Develop as hybrid mobile apps or web apps with iOS or Android (Material) native look and feel. It is also an indispensable prototyping apps tool to show working app prototype as soon as possible in case you need to.

The main approach of the APAM Barabai is to give you an opportunity to create iOS and Android (Material) apps with HTML, CSS and JavaScript easily and clear. APAM Barabai is full of freedom. It doesn't limit your imagination or offer ways of any solutions somehow. APAM Barabai is skeleton to gives you freedom, to change, to share!

As hybrid APP, it is focused only on iOS and Android (Material) to bring the best experience and simplicity.

APAM Barabai is definitely for you if you decide to build (SIMKES Khanza Patient APP) iOS and Android hybrid app (Cordova or PhoneGap) or web app that looks like and feels as great native iOS or Android (Material) apps.
